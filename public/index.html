Find the politicians and donors that are impacting your future

We give you just the facts about your politicians

Poliana is 100% driven by open data!

We bring together millions of data points on the political system: voting records, political donations, lobbyist spending, bill sponsorships, industry influence, and more.

Unlike traditional media, we’re not interested in feeding you a pre-made agenda. We make it our goal to provide you with raw, unaltered information on our government to let you draw your own conclusions.

Using a bi-partisan approach gives the American people a wholistic view on Washington, and that’s what we’re all about.

Learn about the issues that matter most to you

Gain a deeper insight into politicians!

We help you find the politicians and donors that are having the greatest impact on the issues you care about. You can browse the big picture or search for something more specific.

Whether you lean liberal or conservative, we want to connect you to the politicians that most share your political views.

We work with those dedicated to transparency

Our data comes from a variety of political transparency initiatives. We allow only the best, most trusted sources of open-government data for use on Poliana. All information we use comes from those who really care about making a difference in our government.

Share with your friends on social media

Start a conversation!

We think Poliana is sure to spark a much needed conversation in our government, so we’ve made it easy to share the most interesting things you find on the site to social media.

The more you share, the more we can raise awareness about the issues that we face as a nation. That’s progress, not congress.
